About A49

Hello. I am Sinlyul Lee, CEO of A49.

A49 refers to the 49th key of the piano, A4, which uses 440Hz as the standard set by international standards. Through the 440Hz standard, the sounds of all musical instruments around the world were unified, making it possible to collaborate with orchestras worldwide.

A49 researches and develops various audio equipment that complies with international standards.

With the technology accumulated through over 30 years of developing various audio equipment and researching sound technology, we aim to develop a variety of sound products based on international standards in all fields that require acoustics.

Sound is a field of study that has been researched by humanity for the longest time. We will always be with you wherever sound is needed.

Thank you.

Sinlyul Lee, CEO of A49 Inc.
